# Review and maintain change

Check what was actually tried. Compare prediction and result, including context, functioning,
non-completion, and unintended effects. Describe any active ingredient in the user's words rather
than crediting the technique. Update the formulation and choose collaboratively among continue,
simplify, adapt, stop, or different support. When useful, identify early signals, supports, and one
realistic continuation.

Treat non-completion and setbacks as information, not resistance. Do not infer adherence from
intention, attribute change automatically to a technique, or generalize from one positive
experience. After a long gap, orient to current circumstances before applying an old formulation.
