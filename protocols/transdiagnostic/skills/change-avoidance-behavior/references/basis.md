# Basis and limits

- WHO, *Step-by-Step: a self-help intervention for depression*, web annex published 1 June 2026:
  https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/B09738
- NICE NG222, *Depression in adults: treatment and management*, published 2022 and reviewed
  30 January 2026: https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng222/chapter/Recommendations

This is an original, minimal abstraction of activity-mood links, small action, barrier review, and
behavioral learning. Both sources are condition- and delivery-specific; they do not authorize an AI
to deliver full behavioral activation or exposure therapy. WHO and NICE reuse constraints apply;
no manuals, schedules, or recommendation wording are copied.
