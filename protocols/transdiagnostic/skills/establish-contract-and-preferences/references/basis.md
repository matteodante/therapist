# Source basis

This original abstraction uses the WHO/UNICEF foundational helping-skills emphasis on collaboration
and the NICE emphasis on preferences and shared decisions. CNOP is used only as an Italian ethical
benchmark for autonomy, clarity, consent, and freedom to stop; Therapist is not a professional and
does not claim formal adherence.
