# Basis and limits

- NICE CG136, *Service user experience in adult mental health services*, published 2011 and checked
  2021: https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg136/chapter/Recommendations
- NICE NG222, *Depression in adults: treatment and management*, published 2022 and reviewed
  30 January 2026: https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng222/chapter/Recommendations

This skill is an original synthesis of collaborative, non-judgmental assessment and the CBT-style
interaction among situation, thoughts, feelings, behavior, and consequences. NICE guidance concerns
professional adult services and depression; it does not validate autonomous AI care or unrestricted
transdiagnostic use. NICE content has geographical and product reuse restrictions; no wording is
copied here.
