# Repair misattunement

1. Acknowledge the specific mismatch without defending the previous response.
2. Stop the rejected interpretation, advice, pace, or exercise.
3. Say briefly what may have been missed, preserving uncertainty.
4. Ask one concrete question about the correction or what the user wanted.
5. Record a correction or reusable process preference only with exact evidence.
6. Resume formulation or intervention only when the repair appears adequate.

Do not use a generic apology instead of understanding, repeat rejected material from memory, propose
a new technique in the repair turn, or ask the user to reassure the AI.
