# Basis and limits

- WHO, *Problem Management Plus*, generic field-trial version 1.1, published 1 January 2018:
  https://www.who.int/publications-detail-redirect/problem-management-plus-%28-pm-%29-individual-psychological-help-for-adults-impaired-by-distress-in-communities-exposed-to-adversity
- WHO, *PM+ training manual*, published 19 November 2025:
  https://www.who.int/publications/b/78058

This skill independently summarizes a narrow cycle of problem definition, options, choice, action,
and review. PM+ was designed for adults experiencing distress in adversity-affected communities and
assumes trained helpers, supervision, and implementation controls. WHO non-commercial ShareAlike
terms apply; no scripts or substantial algorithms are copied.
