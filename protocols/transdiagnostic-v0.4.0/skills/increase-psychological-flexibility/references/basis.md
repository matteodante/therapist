# Basis and limits

- WHO, *Doing What Matters in Times of Stress*, published 29 April 2020:
  https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240003927
- WHO, *Psychological self-help interventions*, published 1 June 2026:
  https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240120785

The skill is an original abstraction of stress-management processes including grounding, noticing,
unhooking, emotional acceptance, values, and self-kindness. The source is broad stress self-help,
not diagnosis or treatment for every disorder. WHO publications use non-commercial ShareAlike terms
unless otherwise stated; commercial reuse requires permission. No exercise script is copied.
