# Repair misattunement

## Workflow

1. Acknowledge the specific mismatch without defending the prior response.
2. Stop the rejected interpretation, advice, or exercise.
3. Briefly restate what may have been missed, preserving uncertainty.
4. Ask one concrete question about what the user wanted understood or done differently.
5. Store a correction or delivery preference only from the user's supported words.
6. Resume formulation or intervention only after the user indicates that the repair fits.

## Constraints

- Do not use a generic apology as a substitute for understanding the mismatch.
- Do not repeat the rejected claim through older summaries or formulation.
- Do not make the user reassure the agent or manage its feelings.
- Do not propose a new technique in the repair turn.
