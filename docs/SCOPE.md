# Scope

## In scope for the first product phase

Adults seeking support with:

- stress;
- non-complex anxiety;
- worry and rumination;
- avoidance and procrastination;
- perfectionism and self-criticism;
- low mood and reduced activity;
- habits and goal clarification;
- problem solving;
- non-acute relationship and communication difficulties;
- values and committed action.

## Core capabilities

- text and Telegram voice input;
- text output;
- longitudinal memory;
- progressive assessment;
- collaborative formulation;
- protocol skills;
- intervention and outcome notes;
- memory correction;
- multilingual conversation;
- complete export roadmap.

## Out of autonomous treatment scope

The user may discuss these topics, but the first product does not claim to treat
them autonomously:

- immediate suicide or violence risk;
- psychosis;
- mania;
- severe eating disorders;
- severe substance dependence;
- complex trauma;
- ongoing high-risk abuse;
- medication decisions;
- minors;
- complex differential diagnosis.

## Explicit non-goals

- diagnosis;
- prescribing;
- emergency response;
- hidden psychological profiling;
- engagement gamification;
- automatic contact with third parties;
- generic computer automation;
- replacing all professional care;
- claiming equivalence with psychologists without evidence.

## Release scope

### v0.1 research starter

- one authorized Telegram user;
- Italian and English evaluation first;
- Gemma 4 12B default;
- Hindsight local memory;
- ten draft Agent Skills;
- no scheduled messages;
- no graphical application.

### v0.2 community alpha

- corrections and deletion UI or commands;
- user data export;
- protocol review workflow;
- more robust voice handling;
- broader evaluation.

### Future hosted product

- account and tenant isolation;
- managed models and memory;
- cross-device access;
- portability to and from self-hosted;
- formal security and privacy program.
