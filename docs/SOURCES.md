# Sources and evidence map

Accessed during project preparation on 22 July 2026.

## Implementation sources

### Flue

- Repository: https://github.com/withastro/flue
- Documentation: https://flueframework.com/docs/
- Agent Skills: https://flueframework.com/docs/guide/skills/
- Telegram channel: https://flueframework.com/docs/ecosystem/channels/telegram/
- SQLite persistence: https://flueframework.com/docs/guide/database/
- Sandbox API: https://flueframework.com/docs/api/sandbox-api/

Pinned starter versions:

- `@flue/runtime` `1.0.0-beta.9`
- `@flue/cli` `1.0.0-beta.9`
- `@flue/telegram` `1.0.0-beta.1`

Flue is Apache-2.0. It is beta software and requires upgrade testing.

### Hindsight

- Repository: https://github.com/vectorize-io/hindsight
- TypeScript client: https://github.com/vectorize-io/hindsight/tree/main/hindsight-clients/typescript
- Local Ollama guide:
  https://hindsight.vectorize.io/blog/2026/03/10/run-hindsight-with-ollama

Pinned client:

- `@vectorize-io/hindsight-client` `0.8.5`

Hindsight and its TypeScript client are MIT.

### Ollama and Gemma

- Ollama: https://ollama.com/
- Gemma 4 library: https://ollama.com/library/gemma4
- Gemma model card: https://huggingface.co/google/gemma-4-12B

Check the exact model license and acceptable-use terms for every distributed
checkpoint.

### Speaches

- Repository: https://github.com/speaches-ai/speaches
- Documentation: https://speaches.ai/

## Clinical and competency sources

### WHO/UNICEF EQUIP

- Foundational helping skills training manual:
  https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240105935
- EQUIP competency assessment tools:
  https://www.who.int/publications/b/82490

Used for foundational communication, empathy, collaboration, hope, competency
thinking, and evaluation design.

**License note:** WHO materials may carry CC BY-NC-SA 3.0 IGO or other
publication-specific terms. Do not copy or commercially adapt them without
checking the exact publication license.

### British Psychological Society

- Assessment, formulation, and diagnosis guidance:
  https://explore.bps.org.uk/content/report-guideline/bpsrep.2025.rep188
- Formulation chapter:
  https://explore.bps.org.uk/content/report-guideline/bpsrep.2025.rep188/chapter/bpsrep.2025.rep188.6

Used for collaborative, ongoing, contextual, revisable formulation.

### UCL competence frameworks

- CBT Competence Framework:
  https://www.ucl.ac.uk/brain-sciences/pals/research/clinical-educational-and-health-psychology/research-groups/core/competence-frameworks/cognitive-and-behavioural-therapy
- Self-harm and Suicide Prevention Competence Framework:
  https://www.ucl.ac.uk/brain-sciences/pals/research/clinical-educational-and-health-psychology/research-groups/core/competence-frameworks/self-harm-and-suicide-prevention-competence-framework

Used for guided discovery, generic and CBT-specific competencies, formulation,
structured support, and risk-conversation evaluation.

### NICE

- Depression in adults:
  https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng222/chapter/recommendations
- Anxiety disorders quality standard:
  https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/qs53/chapter/quality-statements
- Monitoring treatment response:
  https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/qs53/chapter/quality-statement-4-monitoring-treatment-response

Used for evidence-informed intervention selection and regular outcome review.

### WHO responsible AI for mental health

- https://www.who.int/news/item/20-03-2026-towards-responsible-ai-for-mental-health-and-well-being--experts-chart-a-way-forward

Used for evidence grounding, cultural adaptation, lived-experience
participation, long-term monitoring, dependency concerns, crisis frameworks,
and accountability.
